Visualizing worker activity in time

Open Info

You have reached the worker level page!

On this page are presented statistics about a worker. The scatterplot is showing the worker's activity in time between the first and the last registration. On the y axis the prize of each challenge is showed. When firstly generated, every circle in the chart represent a challenge for which the worker registered. Each challenge is colored based on the type of the challenge (First to Finish, etc).

The chart contains also 2 groups of radio buttons which can help you better understand the worker's behaviour. You can change the color of the circles by selecting another radio button on the left side group and you can filter the challenges by selecting another radio button on the right side group.

The pie chart on the right side of the scatterplot is presenting percentages of challenges depending on what the user has in the scatterplot. Can be percentages of different types of tasks or percentages of submission.
