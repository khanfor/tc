In this page more challenges are presented using small multiples visualization, each of the squares representing a challenge. Each of the squares are including a graphic that shows how the workers registered in time for that specific challenge. Thus, you can also see the number of workers on the verticle axis and the time on the horizantal axis (starting end ending date of the project). Clicking on one of the squares is getting you to the challenge level where you will be able to visualize more information about the challenge.

On the left hand side of the page there is filtering section. You can select multiple filters for searching for the challenges that it are the most interesting you.
